# Will Allred — LinkedIn Posts Collection

## Post 1: Start Conversations, Not Conversions
**Date**: 2024
**Source**: https://www.linkedin.com/in/williamallred/

**Summary**: Allred's core argument from Lavender's data analysis of billions of sales emails: most CTAs focus on driving a conversion (book a meeting, see a demo). Instead, focus on starting a conversation. Questions like "how are you currently handling X?" are much more engaging than "want to see how we can help?"

Lavender's data shows that emails with conversation-starting CTAs get 2x higher reply rates than conversion-focused CTAs.

**Key Insight**: The goal of a cold email isn't to book a meeting. It's to get a reply. Meetings come from conversations, not from CTA optimization.

## Post 2: Data-Backed Email Optimization
**Date**: Ongoing (core Lavender methodology)
**Source**: LinkedIn + levelup.lavender.ai

**Summary**: Allred shares Lavender's findings on what statistically predicts email responses:
- **Reading grade level**: Write at a 5th grade level. Most reps write at 10th+ grade. Lower grade = higher reply rate.
- **Sentence length**: Keep sentences under 12 words on average.
- **Word count**: First cold email should be 25-75 words. Every word above 75 decreases reply probability.
- **Questions**: Include exactly 1 question. Zero questions = no reason to reply. 2+ questions = too much cognitive load.
- **Personalization placement**: First line personalization works. Middle-of-email personalization doesn't move the needle.

**Key Insight**: These aren't opinions — they're statistical patterns from analyzing billions of real sales emails. The data often contradicts conventional "best practices."

## Post 3: The Future Is Agentic Prospecting
**Date**: 2025–2026
**Source**: LinkedIn (promoting Ora.im)

**Summary**: Allred is building Ora.im — agentic prospecting powered by deep research, reasoning, and email analysis from Lavender's dataset. His thesis: the next evolution of cold outreach isn't better templates or faster automation. It's AI agents that research prospects, identify relevant triggers, and draft contextually appropriate emails with minimal human input.

**Relevance**: Represents the direction cold outreach is heading. Current playbooks should account for how AI agents will change the workflow within 12-18 months.
