# Jack Reamer — LinkedIn Posts Collection

## Post 1: The Myth of LinkedIn Impressions = Inbound Leads
**Date**: 2026
**Source**: https://www.linkedin.com/in/jackreamer/

**Summary**: Reamer busted a common myth: "Once I get 25K+ impressions a week, inbound LinkedIn leads will start booking calls with me on autopilot." His experience showed this isn't true. Impressions don't convert to meetings without outbound follow-up. LinkedIn content warms the audience, but you still need to reach out directly.

**Key Insight**: Content is a warmer, not a closer. Use impressions to identify engaged prospects, then reach out with personalized messages referencing their engagement.

## Post 2: Perception Scanning
**Date**: 2026
**Source**: LinkedIn

**Summary**: Before prospects ever reply to your outreach, they've already evaluated you through "perception scanning":
- Your online presence (LinkedIn profile, website)
- Your messaging (does it sound like everyone else?)
- Your reviews/testimonials
- Your positioning

This happens in seconds. If your messaging sounds generic, you become interchangeable. Reamer argues that 80% of outreach failures happen before the prospect reads the email — they looked you up and weren't impressed.

**Key Insight**: Profile optimization is outreach optimization. SalesBread's list-building process takes up to a week because they analyze WHO is buying, identify patterns, and build look-alike prospect lists.

## Post 3: Ultra-Personalized LinkedIn Outreach Results
**Date**: Ongoing (core SalesBread methodology)
**Source**: LinkedIn + SalesBread.com

**Summary**: SalesBread's numbers from 1M+ LinkedIn messages over 5 years:
- 19.98% average reply rate (vs industry average of 7-15%)
- 48.14% of replies are meeting requests or qualified sales inquiries
- No AI-generated messages — all human-written by in-house personalization experts
- Each message references specific details from the prospect's profile, content, or company

Their process: 1 week of list building → ultra-personalized messages → consistent follow-ups → ~1 qualified lead per day per client.

**Key Insight**: "We don't use AI to reach out to prospects." In a world of AI-generated outreach, human personalization at scale (even if lower volume) produces dramatically higher conversion rates. The math works: fewer emails × higher conversion > more emails × lower conversion.
