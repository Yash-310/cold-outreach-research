## Key Episode Summaries

### 1. LinkedIn Post Engagement Scraping as a Prospecting Source
**Date**: 2026

After scraping post engagements from relevant industry posts:
- Enrich the data to filter by ICP criteria
- Exclude competitors before reaching out
- At ~25 connection requests per day: ~100 new connections per week at 70%+ acceptance rate
- Key: the first message should be simple — start a conversation, not pitch

The window to capitalize on this tactic is open now. Jeremy and Jack both emphasize these engagement-based approaches don't stay this effective forever.

### 2. Cold Email Deliverability Deep Dive
**Date**: 2025-2026 (recurring topic)

As QuickMail's founder, Chatelaine has deep infrastructure knowledge:
- Email warmup isn't optional — it's the foundation
- Dedicated IP vs shared IP: for cold email volume under 5,000/month, shared is fine. Above that, dedicated.
- Authentication chain: SPF → DKIM → DMARC → BIMI (in that order of priority)
- Custom tracking domains reduce spam flagging vs default tracking pixels
- Reply rate is the #1 signal to email providers that your messages are wanted

### 3. Single Channel vs Multi-Channel Outreach
**Date**: 2026

Key debate points from recent episodes:
- **Single channel (email only)**: Lower complexity, easier to measure, better for high-volume. Works when your list is strong and ICP is well-defined.
- **Multi-channel (email + LinkedIn + phone)**: Higher conversion, better for enterprise targets. Required when deal sizes justify the extra effort.
- **The hybrid approach**: Lead with whichever channel your prospect is most active on. Use the other channels as fallback/amplification.
- Chatelaine's recommendation: start single-channel to establish a baseline, then layer in additional channels to measure incremental lift.

### 4. Cold Outreach Strategy and Profile Setup for Beginners
**Date**: 2026

For those just starting:
- Optimize LinkedIn profile before ANY outreach (headline, about section, featured content)
- Build prospect lists carefully — avoid inactive users (no posts or activity in 90+ days)
- Outreach starts working once list, strategy, and message are aligned — if any one leg is broken, nothing works
- Best time to send: test your own audience. General benchmarks (Tuesday morning) don't hold for every ICP.
