# Cold Outreach Pipeline for B2B SaaS — Research Repository

## Topic
**Cold outreach pipeline for B2B SaaS** — the end-to-end system of identifying prospects, crafting messages, managing deliverability, and booking qualified meetings through cold email and LinkedIn outreach.

## Why This Topic
Cold outreach remains the most direct lever an early-stage or growth-stage B2B SaaS company can pull to generate pipeline without relying on inbound or paid channels. Understanding the full pipeline — from ICP definition through email infrastructure to reply handling — is foundational for any demand generation role.

## Expert Selection Criteria
I selected 10 practitioners who actively run or coach cold outreach campaigns, not journalists or aggregators. Criteria:

- **Practitioner-first**: Each expert runs real campaigns or coaches teams that do (not just writing about theory)
- **Public teaching**: Active on YouTube, LinkedIn, newsletters, or podcasts with specific, tactical content
- **Recency**: Content from 2024–2026, reflecting current deliverability landscape and AI-driven personalization
- **Diversity of angle**: The list covers cold email copywriting, deliverability infrastructure, LinkedIn outreach, AI automation, multi-channel sequencing, and sales coaching
